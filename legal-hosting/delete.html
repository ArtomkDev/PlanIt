Data Security & Verification

Your data security is our top priority. To prevent unauthorized deletion of your schedules and personal data by third parties, we must verify that you are the legitimate owner of the account.

WARNING: The easiest, fastest, and most secure way to delete your account is to do it directly within the PlanIt app. Navigate to Settings → Account Settings → Delete Account.

How to request account deletion via website:

If you no longer have access to the app, you can send us an email to formally request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

Important Requirements:

  • The email MUST be sent strictly from the exact email address that is linked to your PlanIt account.
  • Requests sent from unverified or mismatched email addresses will be automatically rejected for security reasons.
Send Deletion Request to planit.app.support@gmail.com

Upon verification, your account and all associated User-Generated Content will be permanently removed from our active servers.
